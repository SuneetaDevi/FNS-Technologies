Liquid & hazardous Waste

Most businesses need to dispose of some form of hazardous wastes including: oils, grease trap, acids, wash waters, hydrocarbon waste, solvents,spray booth waste, medical waste and spent powder reagents.

Hazardous waste cannot go in the general waste stream and there are strict regulations on how to dispose of it correctly. Depending on the nature of waste and the risk it poses, it may be harmful if released into the environment untreated..[21]

Liquid waste can be stored in holding tanks or packaged in drums, intermediate bulk containers or approved small containers.

How do we handle hazardous waste?.

FNS Technology’s highly trained and experienced team can safely collect, treat and correctly dispose of your hazardous waste at one of our licensed hazardous waste treatment facilities. Our specialised waste fleet ensures we can collect and dispose of any type of hazardous waste and volumes that your business produces. hazardous waste can be removed via vacuum tanker for bulk volumes or packaged for safe transportation and disposal with our pantech trucks..[21]

Our licensed hazardous waste treatment facilities meet strict Waste disposal act and regulations surroundings and are operated by highly trained and experienced personnel. We have extensive knowledge in the treatment of most hazardous waste types and can develop a solution to meet your disposal needs. FNS Technology provides high quality environmental testing and monitoring using state-of-the-art equipment and provides tracking and documentation to ensure your business complies with regulations..

FNS Technology is committed to resource recovery and works with clients to identify cost-effective treatment solutions which divert hazardous waste from landfills, where possible..

Why choose Veolia for your hazardous waste?.

The safe and effective handling of hazardous waste requires an exceptional level of experience, care and attention to detail. Veolia has a dedicated team committed to providing a professional service to all clients..[21]

We have the capability to collect all volumes of hazardous waste, from small quantities, to bulk containers and large tanks. Our collection services are available 24 hours a day with quick response times and emergency response..

We adhere to the highest standards of safety and compliance to regulations, so you can trust that your hazardous waste is being managed responsibly..
